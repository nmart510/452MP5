Patrol Controls:
  • P: Auto spawning on/off
  • C: Spawns a new patrol
  • B: Draw the bounding box of all patrols
  • J: Trigger the hit animation for head objects
  • H: Apply random velocity to head objects
  • V: Toggle motion of head objects
Camera Controls:
  • 0: Toggle the camera which focuses on hero
  • 1/2/3: Toggle the other zoom camera objects
Hero Controls:
  • Space: Spawn a DyePack at currrent hero position
  • Cursor: Hero will try to follow the cursor
  • Q: Trigger the hit animation for Hero

DyePack Controls:
  • D: Press to slow down all DyePacks
  • S: Trigger hit animation for all DyePacks
